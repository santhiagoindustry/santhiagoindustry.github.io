"BROKEN"

Broken portrays our inner breaking point, our explosion mental, that event within our life that leaves us no more words, but only a tear.

Materials: marble, acrylic, raw copper.

Size: 70 x 50 x 3,5 cm

"ESSENZA"

Essenza depicts the lack we all have in our life, that one same lack that, represented by a gash on the canvas, comes filled completely by a light, the same that serves to illuminate our life.

Materials: wood, aluminum, polycarbonate, acrylic.

Size: 180 x 80 x 3,5 cm

"DEEP"

Deep wants to represent how a person might appear at first glance: anonymous, antisocial, alienated or even lost ... but at the same time how much this person can be truly alive and full of inner resources. Sometimes just a breath exercised in the right way is enough to rekindle the soul.
Remember “never judge a book by its cover.”

Materials: gold leaf, resin, leds between double canvas.

Size: 150 x 80 x 3,5 cm

"MAYBE"

Maybe it's a detailed cast of my forearm and an exact replica of an old photo of it.

Materials: marble, gold powder, resin, adhesives.

Size: 63 x 12 cm

"FAKE"

A 360-degree rotating sculpture, like the faces of the people around us. You are attracted by a mask, by that glow caused by the gold leaf that has the sole purpose of attracting towards you. Over time, the person begins to show themselves for who they really are, causing the glow to slowly disappear.

Materials: polystyrene, gold leaf, resin, adhesives.

Size: 18 x 18 cm

"LIE"

Lie is the representation of the face of a false, liar, who as in reality, it creates a mask that hides the truth. But with over time the mask will melt and eventually the truth will come out.

Materials: slate, polystyrene, acrylic, soy wax.

Size: 32 x 20 x 32 cm

"MYSELF"

Myself tells about our inner strength.
Those who suffer from dispersion and insecurity can understand what it feels like to no longer feel themselves, to no longer feel like one person, but made up of various pieces that are joined by small strands of emotions.
Work made on commission based on the client's emotions.

Materials: resin, gold leaf, wood, acrylic, steel.

Size: 75 x 63 x 20 cm

Who ? am
Gianmario Cordella, aka Santiago, was born in Padua in 2000.After his studies, he worked for 2 years in the family business, dealing in the metal trade hon precious, an important starting point for his artistic career.
In 2016, he begins to devote himself full time to art and the wide world to it connected. The first milestones arrive in 2018, with the exhibition at the Ai Navigli restaurant in Padua and Mazzon Furniture in Torreglia (PD), start of open collaborations, transversal to the various sectors, passing from fashion to interior design. The works of Santhiago is about lived emotions and sensations, deep to the point of having to vent them trying to make them tangible. The artist transmits through his own creations true and own moods to the observer.
Expositions
  • January 2018 “Triptych” at the restaurant “Ai Navigli“ in Padova
  • March 2018 collaboration with Arredamenti Mazzon of Torreglia (PD) with the creation of the lamps “Inferno, Purgatorio, Paradiso“
  • September 2018 collaboration with BStudio Interni di Casalserugo (PD) with the project “Zip“, a design handle that recalls the slider of the same name and aims to combine art and fashion
  • September 2019 “Free Art” free and free art project dedicated to the city of Abano Terme through the placement of works in public parks
  • September 2020 personal exhibition “Emotion” at the Montirone gallery in Abano Terme
  • November 2020 “Help” installation at Prato della Valle Padova
  • March 2021 collaboration with Pataviumart, a leading company in the world of luxury lighting, for the creation of a new line of lamps
  • May 2021 participation with the work “Ecstasy“ in the collective exhibition at the gallery “La Fonderia in Florence“
  • June 2021 personal exhibition “Defects” at the contemporary space “Tindaci“ in Padua
  • September 2021 permanent exhibition at the contemporary space “Tindaci“ in Padua
  • October 2021 participation in the Biennale of contemporary art in Veneto at villa Badoer in Fratta Polesine (RO)
